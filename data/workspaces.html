What Data is Collected

All data that is collected by the Service is stored using Chrome storage. This means that the data is stored in the individual's Google Account if they are logged in, or otherwise, within the browser's local storage. The following data is collected by the Service:

    • Title - The title of the pages of all the open tabs.
